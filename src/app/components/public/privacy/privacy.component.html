Privacy Policy
"succeed! enables coaches and clients to speak via video-calling."
 
When you sign up with succeed! your data will solely be used to schedule and enable video-calls between users who signed up with succeed!
  • Your first name and last name are shared amongst users who signed up with succeed!
  • Your email address is solely used to send you emails of the scheduled video-calls that are established via succeed!
  • Succeed! has the right to remove your data and your account from our application.
  • Data will never and under no circumstances be used by or sold to third parties.
  • Succeed! is not responsible for the content of communication between users. However the scheduled video-calls are always meant to be coaching sessions between users.
  • Succeed! has the right to remove your data and your account from our application.
  • Coaches and their clients that connect via succeed! conduct all their online sessions via the succeed! application. Your account will be removed when we see that you decided to continue coaching sessions through other applications such as Zoom, Teams, and others.
  • All the coaches that signed up with succeed! are certified and contracted to confidentiality and integrity regarding the coaching sessions.
  • Your data are encrypted and secured.
  • This privacy policy is completely separate from the privacy policy of any coach.
