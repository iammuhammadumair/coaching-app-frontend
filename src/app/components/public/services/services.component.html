Personal leadership

Personal leadership and self-mastery are the same thing. It means that you are leading your emotions in stead of having your emotions lead you. And that kind of personal leadership results in being a stable and strong person, whatever life throws at you. Strong in the sense of being able to decide on how you think and react in any given situation. Part of the Invictus poem by William Ernest Henley is quoted a lot. Oprah Winfrey and Prince Harry seem to like the part of the poem too. “I am the master of my faith, I am the captain of my soul “ Personal leadership development and being in charge of your life, result in the life you choose. Your Succeed coach supports you by asking you questions that bring about your unique leadership style. See coaches

Handle stress

Stress in itself is a beautiful human mechanism. Stress indicates us that action is required, we must do or stop doing something. And when that action is noticed by the brains, relaxation can set in. It might happen that relaxation does not get a chance or the space and time to set in. The brain is constantly receiving messages that something is wrong. And that is when a Succeed coach can help you. A Succeed coach helps you to find ways to think differently so that the brain receives messages that tell it relaxation can set in. To change your thinking patterns entails a lot more then sticking positive affirmations and quotes on your fridge. Deep change that sticks in the unconscious thinking will change the way you feel. Affirmations stuck on your fridge remind you of what you want, that is a good start. See coaches. See coaches

Handle emotions

Emotions can feel like storms of rage or sadness. The storm takes over everything. You cannot think properly, and you do things that seem right at that moment but that you might regret afterwards. Your Succeed coach supports you in finding your way to the eye of the storm. In the eye of the storm everything is quiet, you see and hear the effect of the emotions from a place that allows you to reflect. Now you understand what happens, and you can change your thoughts that initiated the emotions in the first place. Handling your emotions by finding the eye of the storm benefits you and the people around you. You communicate in a caring and constructive way. See coaches

Prevent burnout

If you are aware that you might be close to walking into a wall, you are lucky. You feel that you are tired but wonder why, you have not done so much. You go to the gym or for a run to up your physical energy. That helps a little. Things that seemed simple, now seem somewhat complicated. All this indicates that you would do yourself a favor by talking to a Succeed coach who is specialized in burnout. You prevent the real burnout from happening. Burnout literally means that you are burned out. What ever you try to do, the spark that ignites your motor to do that is gone. Preventing burnout means finding out what happens in your unconscious. It means changing your attitude and enhance awareness of your work ethics. Prevent the spark from dying out. Talking with a Succeed coach when you feel something is out of place, proves that you take responsibility in leadership. See coaches

Public speaking

Coaches that specialize in public speaking work with you on many levels. Your posture, your breathing, your voice, tempo, your arms, hands, and legs, they way you feel, and of course also the way you come across. Public speaking for a large group is the same experience as when the group is smaller. A news reader or reporter is different from any other public speaker because the news they bring has nothing to do with their persona. When you share anything else but the news, you want your public to feel attracted to your message. Congruency is key for this. Your physical, emotional and mental parts must be aligned with your message. Your Succeed coach works with you on all these levels so that your message and appearance are compelling to your public. Challenges and Outcomes

When we are confronted with difficulties, those difficulties can be turned around in outcomes. What comes after the difficulty is the result of smart thinking and doing things. Therefor the first thing we do is think of the result that we would like to see, hear, and feel. For example, you feel that you are losing control of your eating habits. If you can imagine, what your voice sounds like when you look in the mirror and how you feel when you tell yourself you are back in the saddle, your outcome is compelling. The outcome is now to find the right attitude to follow up on the challenge. Your Succeed coach supports you in finding that attitude and unique strategy that fits your persona. Any challenge or outcome can be approached in this way. Find the right attitude, and the path to your outcome will be clear to you. See coaches

Relationships

Relating with other people is one of the most basic yet complicated aspects of our lives. We relate with colleagues, parents, children, partners, friends etc. in different ways. Our personal or professional relationships can undermine our sense of wellness sometimes. The relationship eats at us, and we don’t know how to change it. We think that we should communicate differently, we try out different relationship tips and the relationship does not get any better. Or so it seems. Your Succeed coach looks at your relationship as an outsider. They are able to objectively evaluate the dynamics of your relationship. Therefor it is best if all parties come to the coaching sessions. When that is not possible, it will be very difficult to work at bettering your relationships. What is possible though, is that you and your Succeed coach bring about the best in you, in relation with another person. And that is also very helpful to smoothen the relationship. See coaches

Inner conflict

One part of you says “yes” and the other part of you says “no”. Both parts are members of you as a whole person and both want the best for you as a person. Any smart rational thinking process or talk with friends or maybe your boss, seems to be in vain, the conflict is nagging at you, and you postpone decisions or actions. Status quo so to speak, the inner conflict keeps you where you are, stuck in the middle. Your Succeed coach and you together explore what is happening in the unconscious thinking. Rationally you know the pros and cons, unconsciously there is something else going on. Unconscious aspects of yourself are telling you different stories. These stories are about purpose, values, skills, preferences in different situations and so on. Once you have clear what the fuss is about in your unconscious thinking you are able to solve the conflict. See coaches

Quit smoking

And when a nice glass is poured, lighting a cigarette is often the second gesture. To change the habit takes some decision making. Why do you want to quit? What does quitting bring you? More energy, a more tasteful breath, health? And when you have achieved all of that, then what have you achieved for yourself as person? Which characteristic of you, after quitting smoking, is leading you? What is so attractive about that character that you want to give up cigarettes for? You and your Succeed coach will find answers to these questions and lots more. You will find in yourself, the happiness and satisfaction that you are now looking for in a cigarette. See coaches
