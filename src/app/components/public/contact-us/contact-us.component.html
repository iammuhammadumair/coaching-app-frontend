The Amartya foundation in The Netherlands

We support the Amartya Foundation, a Holland based organization that works with communities in Zambia to enhance access to education for everyone.

www.amartya.nl




The Amartya Foundation believes that the best way to contribute to sustainable development is by focusing on education, pride and ownership rather than dependency, volunteerism and aid. As Amartya Sen said: Poverty is not just a lack of money, it is not having the freedom to realize one’s full potential as a human being.


The Amartya Foundation provides school sponsorships to adults and children, because we believe that being able to go to school is a freedom that everybody should have, regardless of age, gender or socio-economic background.
